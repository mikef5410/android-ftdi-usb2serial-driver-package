<H1>Instructions to use:</H1>
select AWG wire size from the drop down list.<br><br>
Press &quot;Show actual wire size&quot; button to view the real wire size.<br><br>
Press back button or &quot;close&quot; button to navigate back from wire size display to main screen.<br><br>
Select &quot;Unit:&quot; menu on the action bar to switch unit between metric and imperial.<br><br>
Single-click on any number to copy it to clipboard.<br>
